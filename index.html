About Me

I’m Winsley Jeune, a software developer blending legacy code with modern tech to build systems that actually work. At Suncor Stainless, I keep critical infrastructure alive while shipping new solutions across React, PHP, Laravel, and AI.

I’ve built an AI embedding system fine-tuned on GPT-2, a trend-scoring engine (MarketMiner), an education resale platform (Ponliv), and even a prototype for real-time threat detection with computer vision.

My journey started in Argentina as a data analyst in 2018. Since then, I’ve gone from WordPress tinkering to full-stack engineering, with a Certified Tech Developer diploma and a near-finished BS in Software Development at BYU.

I’m driven by one thing: solving hard problems with scalable, intelligent software not just writing features, but engineering ecosystems that last.
